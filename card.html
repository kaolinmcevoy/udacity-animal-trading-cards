Maine Coon

Devoted companions, whip-smart, and lively, Maine Coons have been dubbed the dogs of the cat world.

  • Average Length: 1 m (adult, nose to tail)
  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Origin: Maine, United States of America
  • Common Nickname: Gentle giants

Maine coons are built for harsh winters with their bushy tails, double layered coat, and ruff on the chest. They have excellent hunting abilities but are cautious around strangers. They trill, chirp and are generally quite vocal and sometimes have extra toes on their feet!
